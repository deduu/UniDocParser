How to Use PDF Extraction Pro

Follow these simple steps to extract content from your PDFs

1

Upload Your PDF

Drag and drop your PDF file into the upload zone, or click to browse and select your file. Files up to 20MB are supported.

2

Configure Extraction Settings

Choose what content to extract (text, tables, images) and optionally specify page ranges for targeted extraction.

3

Review & Compare

Use the Page Compare view to validate extraction accuracy by viewing the original PDF alongside the extracted content.

4

Download Results

Export your extracted content in JSON or Markdown format for use in your applications or documents.

Pro Tips

  • For best results, use high-quality PDFs with clear text and well-structured tables
  • Use page ranges to extract specific sections and reduce processing time
  • The Preview tab shows how your content will look when rendered as HTML
